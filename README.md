## 📊 Maji Ndogo Data Analysis Dashboard

Data analysis project using Power BI to evaluate water access, service efficiency, safety, crime patterns, and budget allocation in Maji Ndogo.

## 📌 Project Summary
This project analyzes water infrastructure and related systems in Maji Ndogo.

Focus areas:
- Water access  
- Queue times  
- Water quality  
- Crime patterns  
- Budget allocation  

The analysis uses Power BI dashboards to support decisions based on data.

---

## Problem
Communities face:
- Unequal water access  
- Broken infrastructure  
- Long queues  
- Unsafe water sources  
- Uneven resource allocation  

Decisions without data lead to inefficient interventions.

---

## Objective
Answer key operational questions:

1. Where are water access gaps located  
2. Which water sources serve the most people  
3. When do queues peak  
4. Which areas face unsafe water conditions  
5. How resources are allocated across regions  
6. Where interventions deliver the highest impact  

---

## Dashboard Structure

### 1. Water Infrastructure
Focus: access and reliability

- Map  
  Provinces: Sokoto, Hawassa, Kilimani, Amanzi, Akatsi  

- Population split  
  Rural vs Urban  

- Treemap  
  People served per water source  

- Bar chart  
  Source distribution:
  - Well  
  - Tap in home  
  - Tap in home broken  
  - Shared tap  
  - River  

- Stacked bar  
  Water sources per town  

Insight:
- Infrastructure exists but fails in some areas  
- Some populations depend on unsafe sources  

---

### 2. Queues
Focus: service pressure

- Line chart  
  Queue time by hour  

- Bar chart  
  Queue time by day  

- Doughnut chart  
  Queue distribution by gender  

- Bar chart  
  Queue time by province  

Insight:
- Queue time varies by time and location  
- Demand exceeds supply at peak hours  

---

### 3. Water Quality
Focus: safety

- Pie chart  
  - Clean water  
  - Biological contamination  
  - Chemical contamination  

Insight:
- Water access does not ensure safe water  

---

### 4. Crime Data
Focus: social conditions

- Pie chart  
  Crime types by gender  

- Clustered bar chart  
  Gender distribution by province  

- Line charts  
  Crime by hour  
  Crime by day  

Crime types:
- Harassment  
- Theft  
- Sexual assault  
- Public intoxication  
- Severe assault  
- Vandalism  
- Murder  

Insight:
- Crime varies by time and location  

---

### 5. Budget and Improvements
Focus: resource allocation

- Doughnut chart  
  Budget by improvement type  

- Pie chart  
  Budget by Rural vs Urban  

- Bar chart  
  Total improvements required  

- Table  
  Per town:
  - Population  
  - Improvements  
  - Cost per citizen  

Insight:
- Resource distribution differs across regions  
- Cost efficiency varies by location  

---

### 6. Supporting Tables
- Aggregated improvements  
- Total budget per province  

Purpose:
- Validate totals  
- Enable detailed review  

---

## Data Flow Logic

Water infrastructure → Queue time  
Queue time → Access difficulty  
Water quality → Safety risk  
Crime data → Social conditions  
Budget → Intervention strategy  

---

## Key Insights

### Water Infrastructure
- Water access varies by location  
- Different regions depend on different water sources  

### Crime Patterns
- Crime distribution varies by gender and province  
- Time-based patterns highlight peak activity periods  

### Queue Behavior
- Queue times vary by hour and day  
- Some provinces experience longer delays  

### Water Quality
- Presence of contaminated water sources  
- Clean water access is not uniform  

### Budget Allocation
- Budget distribution varies by region and project type  
- Town-level data supports targeted investment
